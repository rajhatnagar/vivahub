Getting Started

How do I create my first digital invitation? expand_more

Creating your invitation is simple. Start by selecting 'Create Invitation' from the dashboard. Browse our collection of luxury templates, select your favorite, and use our customization studio to add your event details, photos, and personalized messages.

Can I customize the Indian cultural motifs? expand_more

Absolutely. Our premium templates feature a wide range of traditional and modern Indian motifs (like Paisleys, Mandalas, and Ganeshas) that can be recolored, resized, and repositioned to perfectly match your wedding theme and color palette.

Can I invite guests via WhatsApp? expand_more

Yes, WedLuxe integrates seamlessly with WhatsApp. You can share a personalized link or a digital card image directly to your guests' WhatsApp numbers. We also provide tracking so you know when they've viewed the invitation.

Payments & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept? expand_more

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, and UPI payments for our Indian customers. All transactions are processed securely through Stripe.

Is there a refund policy for premium templates? expand_more

Due to the digital nature of our products, we generally do not offer refunds once a template has been downloaded or shared. However, if you experience technical issues, our concierge team is available 24/7 to assist or issue a refund if the product is defective.

Privacy & Security

Is my guest list data secure? expand_more

Security is our top priority. All guest data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard AES-256 encryption. We never sell your data to third parties.

Can I password protect my wedding website? expand_more

Yes, all premium plans include the option to password protect your wedding website and digital invitations, ensuring only your invited guests can view the details.
